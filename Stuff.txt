Difficulty levels:
On each change, unopened chests re-roll

0 - 5 mins: Starter; enemies killable in 1/2 hits most. Mainly weak ground enemies that don't attack often. Some slow turrets

5 - 15 mins: Easy; Enemies take a couple of hits, start trying to hit the player but slowly. Standard flying enemies appear but are slow and weak

15 - 25 mins: Medium; Swarms start to appear. Easier enemies start to have unblockable fire.

25 - 35 mins: Hard; Swarms are more likely to assign a new leader. More enemies have unblockable fire.

35 - 40 mins: Very Hard; TBC

45 mins onward: Nightmare; TBC
